Baked Ziti

Print Recipe

Ingredients

1 lbs
Ziti noodles
12 oz
Skim ricotta
12 oz
Low fat sour cream
1 lbs
Mozzarella cheese
1
Egg (beaten)
1 jar
Pasta sauce
1/4 cup
Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Make the pasta.
  2. Mix pasta, ricotta, sour cream, mozzarella, egg and most of pasta sauce in a bowl.
  3. Pour bowl contents into a sprayed pan.
  4. Cover with the rest of the sauce and then parmesan cheese.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes at 375°F.

California Pizza Kitchen Homemade Pizza

Print Recipe

Ingredients

1 pack
Yeast
2 cups + 2 tsp
Warm water
2 cups
Bread flour
3 cups
Wheat flour
2 tbsp + 4 tsp
Honey
2 tsp
Salt
2 tbsp
Olive oil
1 jar
Tomato sauce
Some
Cheese(s) of choice

Steps

  1. Put flours, salt, honey and oil into a mixing bowl.
  2. Meanwhile mix yeast and warm water in a separate bowl carefully with your finger.
  3. Dump yeast mixture into main bowl and mix until dough forms.
  4. Split dough into two balls and put in greased pan. Cover and let rise for 1-2 hours.
  5. Roll dough ball out and add sauce, cheese, other topppings and spices.
  6. Bake for 10 minutes at 425°F.

Carrabba's Chicken and Fettuccine Alfredo

Print Recipe

Ingredients

2
Chicken breasts (boneless and skinless)
3 tbsp
Balsamic vinegar
2 tbsp
Extra virgin olive oil
2 tbsp
Fresh basil leaves
"for taste"
Salt and pepper
2 tbsp
Butter
1
Shallots (medium)
1 tsp
Garlic (minced)
1/4 cup (may need more)
Parmesan cheese (grated)
1 cup
Half and half or heavy cream
1/2 cup
Frozen peas
1/2 (9oz) pack
Fresh fettuccine

Steps

  1. Combine vinegar, oil and basil. Pour over chicken and marinate overnight in the fridge.
  2. Grill chicken or bake for 30 minutes at 350°F. Let it rest.
  3. Peel and finely chop shallots. Saute in butter for 1 minute. Add garlic and saute until fragrant.
  4. Add cream, salt and pepper.
  5. Boil to reduce slightly to light sauce consistency (a bit liquidy).
  6. Add peas and parmesan cheese and cook another 2 minutes.
  7. Cook pasta and thinly slice chicken.
  8. Toss pasta with sauce and add chicken.

Grandma's Meatballs and Tomato Sauce

Print Recipe

Ingredients

3 lbs
Meat mix (pork, beef and veal)
1 tsp (may need more)
Salt
1 cup
Pecorino romano cheese
1/2 head
Parsley (finely chopped)
2
Eggs
1/2 loaf
Italian bread
≤ 1/4 cup
Milk
12 cloves
Garlic (minced)
6 cans (28oz)
Cento San Marzano peeled tomatoes
3 cans
Cento Pomato puree

Steps

  1. First make the meatballs (about 50).
  2. Cut bread into small cubes and soak them in water (squeeze out).
  3. Add meats, 1 tsp of salt, cheese, parsley, eggs, bread, milk and garlic in a large bowl. Squish together.
  4. Scoop into mini muffin pan and bake for 30 minutes at 375°F.
  5. Set aside to add to sauce. Now make the sauce.
  6. Hand grind the tomatoes in a large pot. Add the tomato puree and salt for taste.
  7. Heat sauce until almost boiling. Turn down to simmer. Add meatballs.
  8. Cook for 8 hours on low with the lid mostly on. Stir every hour.

Steak with Rosemary Port Sauce

Print Recipe

Ingredients

1 tbsp
Olive oil
2 tsp
Worcestershire sauce
2
Steaks (thin)
1 tbsp
Butter
1 sprig
Rosemary
1 clove
Garlic (big and minced)
1/4 cup
Ruby port

Steps

  1. Mix oil and worcestershire sauce in a bowl. Smear mixture over steaks.
  2. Heat heavy-based frying pan over medium-high heat and cook steaks for 3 minutes per side.
  3. Wrap steaks in aluminum foil and rest on a warm plate while you make the sauce.
  4. While pan is still hot, turn down to low, add butter, let it melt, finely chop rosemary and add it to the pan.
  5. Add garlic to the pan, pour in the port and reduce it slightly.
  6. Unwrap steaks on serving plates. Use sauce as wanted.
