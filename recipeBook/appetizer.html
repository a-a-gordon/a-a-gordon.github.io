Chicken Caesar Salad

Print Recipe

Ingredients

8 oz
Chicken breast (skinless)
4 tsp
Olive oil
4 slices
Baguette (on diagonal, melba thin)
1 clove
Garlic (minced)
2 heads
Baby romaine lettuce
1
Large egg
1 handful
Arugula and mustard cress
2 tbsp
Parmesan (grated)
1 tbsp
Sunflower seeds
2 tbsp
Greek yogurt (fat free)
1 tsp
Dijon mustard
1 tsp
Malt vinegar
Sprinkle
Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350째F.
  2. Brush the chicken with 1 tsp of the olive oil.
  3. On a hot grill pan, sear the chicken breast for 4-5 minutes before flipping over and doing the same for the other side.
  4. While the chicken is searing, slice baguette on diagonal and place on a baking sheet. Brush a little olive oil. Toast the bread for 10 minutes until crisp.
  5. Rub the bread with half of the minced garlic.
  6. Once the chicken has strong grill marks, cover the pan with foil and place in the hot oven on a higher shelf for 15 minutes.
  7. Meanwhile, wash and spin dry lettuce.
  8. Carefully drop the egg into boiling water and boil for 5 minutes. Empty the boiling water and replace it with cold water. Let the egg stand in the cold water as you finish everything else.
  9. Combine yogurt, the remaining half clove of garlic, dijon mustard, 3 tbsp of olive oil, and malt vinegar in a small mixing bowl.
  10. Stir together with whisk and add salt and pepper for taste. Set aside. Remove chicken from oven when done.
  11. Chop lettuce and place in a big bowl. Toss with dressing for a couple minutes to evenly distribute. Cut chicken into strips and toss with salad.

Italian Wedding Soup

Print Recipe

Ingredients

1 tbsp
Extra virgin olive oil
1
Small onion (chopped)
3
Carrots (finely chopped)
2 cloves
Garlic (minced)
2 tsp
Worcestershire sauce
2 tsp
Fresh sage (finely chopped)
3 cups
Chicken broth
1
Parmesan cheese rind
1 tbsp
Grated parmesan
1/2 lbs
Ground pork
3 tbsp
Panko breadcrumbs
3/4 cup
Orzo pasta
8 oz (abt 8 cups)
Baby spinach

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Add onions and carrots, cooking until slightly softened (about 4 minutes).
  2. Add half of the garlic, 1 tsp of the Worcestershire sauce and 1 tsp of the sage. Cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add the chicken broth, 3 cups of water and the cheese rind. Bring to a boil, cover and reduce heat to medium.
  4. Simmer until the vegetables are tender (about 7 minutes).
  5. Meanwhile, mix the pork, breadcrumbs, grated parmesan, the remaining garlic, the remaining Worcestershire sauce and remaining sage in a bowl.
  6. Form 1-inch meatballs. Increase the heat to medium high and bring the soup to a boil.
  7. Stir in the orzo and cook 6 minutes. Add the meatballs and cook until they are firm and float to the top (about 4 more minutes).
  8. Stir in the spinach and cook until wilted (about 1 more minute). Ladle the soup into bowls and top with parmesan.

Jill's Bread Bowl

Print Recipe

Ingredients

1
Bread loaf (large circle)
1 8oz pack
Shredded cheddar cheese
2 3oz packs
Cream cheese
1 1/2 cups
Sour cream
1 cup
Cooked ham (diced)
1/2 cup
Green onions (chopped)
1 tsp
Worcestershire sauce
2 tbsp
Vegetable oil
1 tbsp
Butter melted

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350째F.
  2. Cut a circle into the top of the bread. Remove the top and set aside.
  3. Hollow out the loaf, reserving insides for later dipping.
  4. In a medium bowl, mix the cheddar cheese, cream cheese, sour cream, ham, green onions, and worcestershire sauce.
  5. Spoon into the bread bowl and place the top back on. Wrap the loaf tightly in tin foil and place on a baking sheet.
  6. Bake until cheese is melted and bubbly (about 1 1/2 hours).
  7. Meanwhile, cut reserved bread into small cubes. Toss with oil and melted butter. Place on baking sheet and toast in oven until golden brown (about 10 to 15 minutes).

Twice Baked Potatoes

Print Recipe

Ingredients

8
Large potatoes
1 stick
Butter
1/2 cup
Heavy cream or milk
1 8oz pack
Cream cheese
Some
Shredded cheddar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400째F.
  2. Wash but do not peel potatoes. Stab 3 holes in each potato with a fork.
  3. Put potatoes on tray and bake them for an hour.
  4. When soft, take out of the oven and cut in half long ways, careful not to break the shell.
  5. Use a spoon to scoop out the insides into a bowl. Place shells back on tray and preheat oven to 350째F.
  6. Add room temperature butter and cream cheese to the potato insides.
  7. Microwave the heavy cream for a minute and then add to mixture. Beat it all together with mixer.
  8. Put mashed potatoes back in shells and bake 20 minutes.
  9. Add cheddar cheese to top and bake a few more minutes to melt the cheese.
